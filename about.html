Who We Are

MediChain Health is a healthcare technology startup that combines Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence, and Cloud Computing to create secure digital healthcare solutions.

Our mission is to eliminate medical record fraud, improve interoperability among hospitals, and give patients complete control over their health information.

Our Mission

To build a secure healthcare ecosystem where patients and providers trust digital health records powered by blockchain.

Our Vision

Become the world's most trusted blockchain healthcare platform.
